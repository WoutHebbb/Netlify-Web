In the beginning

Planning and trip to Lithuania

I already had the idea to go international for a long time. When I heard about the MoveOn program I immediately signed up for it. This meant that Thomas More would look for an available internship in Europe with Erasmus+. After some time I got to meet some people from Kauno Kolegija. They would like to offer me an internship with one of their partners in Lithuania. After I signed the contract online, I started to plan my trip to Lithuania. I found a plane I could take, I found a dorm to stay in and I found somebody that would help me around in the city.

Project at Kauno Kolegija

When I arrived my internship company was not ready yet so the College provided my with a project to keep me busy. I helped them test out their new GPON switches and I research about Own Cloud. They want to install Own Cloud and use it in the college. My internship company contacted me during my research. I had a call and after that I had a meeting with them online.

Festo Internship

Building the chatbot in ServiceNow

Now the real fun begins. Festo bought the rights to use ServiceNow. It is a SAAP where users can create tickets, search in a database and more. My job was to make a POC for the build in chatbot. I first learned the basics on their LearnNow platform. Here I earned badges by completing courses. At the end I even got a certificate for the Chatbot.
We made sure I always had 1 or 2 tasks at the time. These tasks where added every week when we finished our sprints. My biggest tasks I finished were:

  • Live agent take over
  • Translation with Deepl
  • Ticket Creations
