    I am Wout Hebberecht. A age Year old Cloud & CyberSecurity Student at Thomas More Geel.

    My Story

    I was born on April 2002 in Geel. This was the start of me sharing my birthday with my dad. When I was in primary school, I started doing sports. I did archery with my father but stopped after 1 year. I played Badminton with my big brother but also stopped after 1 year. After those 2 sports I started with volleyball and I still play it till this day. I even found my student job in my club. I became a referee in 2020.

    My Education

    I started my IT Education in 2018 when I learned the basic of Programming in C# and started working with Virtual Box. But my real Education started in September 2020 in the IT Factory on Thomas More Geel. In the first few weeks, I noticed that I liked the Cisco lessons. That is why I started with the Cloud & Cyber Security course. It may not be the best method to choose a course, but it is the one I got.

    What in the future?

    I want to make my education useful in real life. I am planning to take a second bachelor in the event sector. They are becoming more digital and I believe I can combine the 2 pretty well.
    In my personal life, I still hope I will play volleyball in the same club as I do now. And I want to be healthy, of course.

    International

    I like to explore the world and learn from all kinds of different people with different idea's. That is also why I went on Erasmus myself. You can read more about this on the internship page.
    But it is very expensive and time-consuming to travel abroad every time. That is why I joined ESN (Erasmus Student Network) and became a buddy here in Geel. I help international students settle in this beautiful country, and they tell me all about their country. It is a win-win situation.
